AFTERNOON

It's hot and unbearable. South India does not offer a lot many place to enjoy in a winter afternoon.
You feel it is still summer. And sitting in the class, you are lost in the idea that you might as well
visit some hill station this weekend instead of lying in your hostel, and feel eager to ask others to come with you.

.
.

Your trance is broken by the class bell. Your friend asks you to wake up, the lecturer has gone already.
You decide to head back to your hostel room. Your plans are too big to fail. Tomorrow is not a very important day.
And it is weekend the day after.
You sit by your laptop and check out the near-by hill stations. Ooty catches your attention.
Ooty it is!!!
You close the laptop. Tomorrow, you shall let everyone know that they were all heading to Ooty.
Because you are well aware, everyone will follow. They are too bored at this place, just like yourself.
You head out for lunch.
